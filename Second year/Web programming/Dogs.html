Dogs

Why we love them

      Dogs exemplify loyalty. Beyond companionship, dogs are also fiercely loyal to their humans. It's almost as if, not only do we humans take on the labels of fur parents, but dogs treat us as family as well. They stay by our side and, in their own way, care about us.
